This is a mathematical model of the Dopamine Ultradian Synaptic Regulator (DUSR) developed in the paper  

"A mathematical model for the role of dopamine-D2 self-regulation in the production of ultradian rhythms"

by An Qi Zhang, Adam R. Stinchcombe, & Martin R. Ralph



The model is available in the format of Matlab (*.m) and XPPAUT (*.ode).
